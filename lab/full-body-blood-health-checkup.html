WHOLE BODY HEALTH CHECK-UP

A Whole Body health Check-up (Blood Test) is a package of various tests which intends to assist the holistic assessment of the blood systems, hormones and vital organs of the body such as Heart, Lungs, Kidneys, Liver, pancreas. Overnight fasting is required to be done before few tests.

RECOMMENDED FOR

Those seeking comprehensive evaluation of major systems of the body and individuals who proactively keep a check on their fitness.

AGE GROUP (Recommended)

Separate packages are recommended for different age groups.

FREQUENCY

It is required to be done once in a year or as advised by the Doctor.
